Avaliable Tables



Identity:
Available Tables is an app that allows users to find and book available tables in any restaurant.

Problem:
Many people have anxiety when calling restaurants to book a reservation. However, without calling to make a reservation, they run the risk of having no available tables at the restaurant.

Solution:
With this app, users will be able to book a table without having to make a call. The app will provide an overview of the restaurantâ€™s available and unavailable tables during specific time intervals. Users will then be able to select which table they want to book during which time.

Target Market:
Adults or children that have conset to use the app from a guardian 18 or over.

Competition:
Apps with similar ideas including OpenTable, TheFork, and Resy.

Revenue Streams:
Available Tables will be sold directly to users using the app.

Marketing Activities:
Available Tables will communicate with users through email and targeted ads on social media.

Expenses:
There will be expenses associated with obtaining materials to create the app as well as advertising.

Team and Key Roles:
Currently there are two members, but as the team grows we will need people able to add in restaurants, program booking tables, graphic designers, and advertisers.

Milestones:
As the app grows, Available Tables will advertise in target markets and grow as an efficient way to book tables.
